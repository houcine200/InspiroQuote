Fetch Quotes

All you need to do is send a request to https://inspiroquote.me/api/[mode], where:

  • [mode] = retreval type [catgory, author, daily_quote]

Sample Requests

  • All Quotes:
    Endpoint: https://inspiroquote.me/api/v1/quotes
    Description: Retrieve a JSON representation containing all available quotes within the InspiroQuote database.
  • Quote of the Day:
    Endpoint: https://inspiroquote.me/api/v1/daily_quote
    Description: Obtain the quote of the day in JSON format, dynamically updated to provide daily inspiration.
  • All Categories:
    Endpoint: https://inspiroquote.me/api/v1/categories
    Description: Access a JSON array listing all available categories for categorizing quotes within the InspiroQuote database.
  • Quotes by Category:
    Endpoint: https://inspiroquote.me/api/v1/categories/[id]/quotes
    Description: Retrieve a JSON array containing quotes filtered by a specific category identified by its unique ID.
  • All Authors:
    Endpoint: https://inspiroquote.me/api/v1/authors
    Description: Fetch a JSON array listing all authors contributing quotes within the InspiroQuote database.
  • Quotes by Author:
    Endpoint: https://inspiroquote.me/api/v1/authors/[id]/quotes
    Description: Retrieve a JSON array containing quotes authored by a specific individual identified by their unique ID.

About InspiroQuote

InspiroQuotes was born out of a shared passion for words and inspiration among our team members. It all began with a simple idea: to create a platform where people could easily access and share inspirational quotes that resonate with them.

As individuals, we've always been drawn to the power of words to uplift, motivate, and provoke thought. Whether it's a quote from a renowned philosopher, a famous author, or an influential leader, we believe that these snippets of wisdom have the potential to spark positive change in people's lives.

Throughout the development process, we've poured our hearts and souls into crafting an experience that not only provides access to a vast collection of quotes but also fosters a sense of community and inspiration.

This project is not just a requirement for ALXafrica; it's a labor of love and a testament to our dedication to spreading positivity and wisdom in the world.
